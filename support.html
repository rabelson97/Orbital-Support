Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do focus sessions work?
A: Set your desired focus time and Orbital breaks it into 5-minute planet generation intervals. Each interval creates a unique planet while you focus on your work.

Q: Can I pause a session?
A: Yes, sessions automatically pause when you leave the app and resume when you return.

Q: Where are my completed planets stored?
A: All your generated planets are saved locally on your device and can be viewed in your planet collection.
